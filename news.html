    Bootcamp  theme
    The pandemic years have showcased that the power of accessibility and technology really keeps our societies moving. At Stockholm Innovation Bootcamp we not only want to bring people together from a wide range of fields - we want to provide challenges that can resonate with a wide range of fields! The theme for this year's Bootcamp is access to justice and social impact. What really brings people to innovate is a sense of rebelling against the status quo, and legal certainly is one area stuck in a status quo mentality.
