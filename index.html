Welcome to Stockholm Startup Bootcamp

Would you like to experience problem solving in action?


  • Hands-on experience in interdisciplinary teams
  • The legal, business and tech field joining forces
  • Real challenges, real solutions


Stockholm Startup Bootcamp is an interdisciplinary hands-on workshop on entrepreneurship, design thinking and innovation.


During the intense 3-day bootcamp, students will get to experience what design thinking and innovation can be in practise and learn from industry veterans by being faced with real challenges from industry entities and government branches. At the end of the camp, the solutions are pitched to stakeholders whom will offer feedback on the work.


The Bootcamp offers a great opportunity for any student to expand networks and put own field knowledge into practise, whilst being introduced to the world of startups.

For whom?

Students with focus on law, technology, software engineering or business at Stockholm University, Stockholm School of Economics and the Royal Institute of Technology. Both Bachelor and Master Level students are welcomed, regardless of semester.


Students at other universities are also welcome to apply! Our primary focus is, however the aforementioned universities.


Exchange/International student? You are also welcomed to apply like your swedish fellows!


No prior knowledge is required, only a curiosity about tech and innovation. The Bootcamp is held in English, so a good understanding of, and vocabulary in, English is beneficial. Don't worry; "badenglish" works too!

Agenda - 24th-26th of January 2020

  • Day 1 - Meet your interdisciplinary team and get introduced to the challenge.
  • Day 2 - Lecture on Design Thinking and around the challenge. Work with your solution in your team, supported by our mentors!
  • Day 3 - Finalize your solution, lecture on pitching and time to meet the judges!
